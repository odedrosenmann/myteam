Maccabi Tel Aviv

Maccabi Tel Aviv is a professional basketball club based in Tel Aviv, Israel. The team plays in the Israeli Premier League and internationally in the Euroleague

Visit the team's website

With six European Championships, one Adriatic Championship, 51 Israeli Championships, 42 Israeli Cups, and six League Cups, Maccabi has been the most successful basketball team in Israel. It is also one of the most successful teams of the European basketball in all times.
